Church Leadership

A body without a head is incomplete so is an organization without any form of leadership. The church's leadership is centered around the priest in charge, who presides over a Pastoral Council made up of members of the community. Their task is to ensure the smooth running of St Bakhita Catholic Church.

Rev Fr Hillary Agbenosi

Rector, Priest in Charge

Fr. Hillary joined St. Bakhita as its first rector in the year of its dedication, 2018.

Church Pastoral Council

Church Youth Council

The Youth Council, headed by Ms Rebecca Bataka, represents the interests and guides the energies of the youth of the church towards the greater good of the community.
