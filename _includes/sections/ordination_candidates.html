The Candidates

All candidates are alumni of St Paul's Seminary, Sowutuom, and St Peter's regional seminary, Pedu. They completed their Philosophy and Theology studies at St Paul's and St Peter's respectively.
Together, they are the highest number of candidates to be ordained in the Catholic Archdiocese of Accra.
St Kizito Parish, Nima, presents the highest number of candidates at this year's ordination.
